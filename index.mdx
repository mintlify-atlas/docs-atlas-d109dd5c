    <Step title="Create an account">
      Contact the administrator via WhatsApp to request access. Each account is limited to one active device at a time for security.
      
      <Note>
        Single-device sessions are enforced automatically. If you log in from a new device, your previous session will be terminated.
      </Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Log in to the platform">
      Navigate to the landing page and enter your credentials. After successful authentication, you'll be redirected to the main dashboard.
      
      ```html
      https://your-ripper-instance.com/
      ```
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Browse the catalog">
      Explore music organized by categories, tonality, and artist. Use the search functionality to quickly find specific tracks.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Start streaming">
      Click any track to open the music player. Enjoy synchronized video playback with keyboard shortcuts for precise control.
      
      <Tip>
        Press **Space** to play/pause, **←/→** for ±5s, **R** to restart, and **F** for fullscreen.
      </Tip>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
