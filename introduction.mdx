## What is Ripper?

Ripper is a **web-based music streaming platform** designed specifically for Peruvian huayno music. It provides a Netflix-style interface that allows musicians and enthusiasts to stream huayno songs while viewing synchronized sheet music and partituras.

<Note>
  Ripper focuses on huayno music in the **Huaraz style**, featuring traditional Peruvian bands and regional variations across different tonalities.
</Note>

## Platform Architecture

Ripper is built as a modern single-page application with:

- **Frontend**: Vanilla JavaScript with modular ES6 imports
- **Backend**: Firebase Authentication and Firestore for user management
- **Streaming**: YouTube and Google Drive integration for audio playback
- **Styling**: Custom CSS with a cinematic, streaming-platform aesthetic

## Music Catalog

Ripper features **86+ huayno songs** from renowned Peruvian bands including:

- Banda Show La Huaranchal
- Banda Centro Musical Pomapata
- Banda Orquesta Social Huancaya (BOSH)
- Banda Jazz Perú Sunicancha
- Banda Perú Armonía y Clase (PAC)
- And many more...

<Warning>
  Access to the full music catalog requires a premium account. Contact the administrator via WhatsApp to request access.
</Warning>
